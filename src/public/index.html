      How to Play Thousand

      Goal

      Be the first player to reach 1000 points.

      Bidding

      Players bid the number of points they expect to score, from 100 to 300 in steps of 5. The highest bidder becomes the declarer and takes the face-down talon cards.

      Selling

      If the declarer can't or won't keep the contract, the hand may be sold to another player (minimum 105, up to 3 attempts).

      Card values

      • Ace — 11
      • Ten — 10
      • King — 4
      • Queen — 3
      • Jack — 2
      • Nine — 0

      Trick ranking (high to low)

      Ace, Ten, King, Queen, Jack, Nine. The Ten beats the King and Queen; the Ace is highest.

      Marriages

      A King + Queen of the same suit, declared on your lead, sets the trump suit and scores a bonus: ♣ 100, ♠ 80, ♥ 60, ♦ 40.

      Trick play

      Follow the led suit if you can. If you can't follow, play a trump if you have one. The highest card wins the trick — trumps beat all plain suits.

      Scoring

      The declarer scores +bid if they make their contract and −bid if they miss it. Everyone else scores the points in the tricks they captured, rounded to the nearest 10.

      On the barrel

      Once your score reaches 880 (and is still under 1000) you are "on the barrel". If you fail to climb past 1000 within 3 rounds on the barrel, you are penalised −120 and drop back down.

      Four nines

      If you are dealt all four nines in your hand, you immediately score a +100 bonus for that round.

      Three zeros

      Scoring zero three rounds in a row costs you −120.
